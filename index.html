MedTrack

An offline-first medication tracker for iPhone.

MedTrack is an iOS app for tracking medication dispensing schedules, dose logs, and remaining supply — for you and your pets. It integrates optionally with Apple Health via the iOS 26 Medications API. All your data stays on your device — there is no backend, no analytics, and no tracking.
