# MedTrack

A simple iPhone app for tracking medication dispensing schedules, dose logs, and remaining supply.

## Links

- [Privacy Policy](./privacy.html)
- [Support](./support.html)

## About

MedTrack is an offline-first medication tracker for iOS 26+. It integrates optionally with Apple Health via the iOS 26 Medications API. All your data stays on your device — there is no backend, no analytics, and no tracking.
